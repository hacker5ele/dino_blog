Overview

The Indominus Rex is a genetically engineered hybrid dinosaur featured in Jurassic World (2015). Created by Dr. Henry Wu, this hybrid combines DNA from multiple species, including T. rex, Velociraptor, and cuttlefish. It was designed as a larger, more terrifying version of a predator to draw crowds and boost park profits.

Physical Characteristics

The Indominus Rex was over 50 feet long and weighed about 8 tons. Its skin was white, and it had the ability to camouflage, making it an even more dangerous predator. Its sheer size and intelligence made it the park's most terrifying creation.
