⚡ Claire Dearing

The leader who steps up to protect the dinosaurs and the park.

Profile

Name: Claire Dearing

Age: 32 (as of *Jurassic World*)

Occupation: Park Operations Manager

Specialty: Theme Park Management

Character Background

Claire Dearing is the operations manager for Jurassic World, the new dinosaur theme park built on Isla Nublar. Initially portrayed as a corporate-minded executive, Claire undergoes significant development, showing a deeper understanding of the park's impact on the dinosaurs and taking charge to protect them during a crisis.

Key Appearances

  • Jurassic World (2015)
  • Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018)
  • Jurassic World: Dominion (2022)

Famous Quote

"We’re not just here to observe, we’re here to save them." – Claire Dearing, Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom (2018)
