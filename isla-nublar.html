Overview

Isla Nublar is the main setting for Jurassic Park and the heart of the franchise. It is the place where InGen created Jurassic Park, the original dinosaur theme park. The island was equipped with high-tech facilities and attractions, but it eventually led to disaster.

Notable Events

From the opening of the park to the breakout of the dinosaurs, Isla Nublar has seen more than its fair share of chaos. The disastrous events of *Jurassic Park* (1993) and later *Jurassic World* (2015) show the dangers of playing with nature.
