Overview

Isla Sorna is the second island in the Jurassic Park franchise, located near Isla Nublar. It was the site of illegal dinosaur breeding and a dangerous place where scientists and explorers had to face the unpredictable nature of these ancient creatures.

Events on Isla Sorna

After the fall of Jurassic Park, Isla Sorna became the site of new experiments and the breeding ground for dangerous new dinosaurs. It is featured in *The Lost World* and *Jurassic Park III*, where different groups venture to the island with varying objectives, leading to chaos and close encounters with deadly dinosaurs like the Spinosaurus.
