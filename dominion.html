Legacy Meets Modern Chaos

Dominion marks the return of fan favorites Alan Grant, Ellie Sattler, and Ian Malcolm. Their reunion intersects with the ongoing journey of Owen Grady and Claire Dearing as they try to protect Maisie Lockwood and uncover corporate secrets behind biosynthetic power.

The film blends heart-pounding action with questions about genetic ethics, environmental balance, and human interference in nature.

Behind the Scenes

Directed by Colin Trevorrow, Dominion was largely shot during the height of the COVID-19 pandemic and became one of the first major Hollywood productions to resume with strict safety protocols. The production team incorporated more animatronics than in previous films to blend practical and digital effects.

Reception & Legacy

Critics were divided, praising the nostalgic reunions and new dinosaurs, while noting a sprawling plot with many moving parts. Fans appreciated the thematic full-circle journey and visual spectacle. Whether seen as a satisfying conclusion or an ambitious crossover, Dominion left a definitive mark on the franchise.
