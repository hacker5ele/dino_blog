Character Background

John Hammond is the founder and CEO of InGen, the company behind the creation of Jurassic Park. He is a wealthy and ambitious man whose dream is to bring dinosaurs back to life and create a theme park where people can experience them. While his vision is groundbreaking, it comes with unforeseen risks and consequences.
