Overview

The Indoraptor is a hybrid dinosaur created in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom. It was engineered as a weapon, combining the traits of both the Velociraptor and the Tyrannosaurus Rex, making it a highly intelligent and formidable predator. With its predatory instincts and stealthy movements, the Indoraptor was designed to be the ultimate weapon of destruction.

Physical Characteristics

The Indoraptor is slender and sleek, with black scales and glowing yellow eyes, making it an intimidating and highly visible predator. It stands at about 10 feet tall and is highly agile, capable of swift movements and climbing. Its long claws and sharp teeth were designed to kill with precision and speed.

In Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom

In the film, the Indoraptor was created by Dr. Wu to be sold as a weapon. It quickly becomes a terrifying force within the mansion, where it hunts humans and other creatures. Its intelligence and hunting abilities make it a unique and dangerous hybrid in the Jurassic Park universe.
