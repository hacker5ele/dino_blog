Profile

Name: Dr. Ian Malcolm

Age: 62 (as of *Jurassic World: Dominion*)

Occupation: Chaos Theorist

Specialty: Mathematical Modeling and Chaos Theory

Character Background

Dr. Ian Malcolm is a renowned chaos theorist, best known for his expertise in mathematical modeling and his controversial theories about unpredictability in complex systems.

Malcolm is portrayed by Jeff Goldblum in the film adaptations, where his witty and charismatic personality shines. He plays a central role in the events of *Jurassic Park*, *The Lost World: Jurassic Park*, and *Jurassic World: Dominion*.
